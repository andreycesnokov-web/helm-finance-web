# Helm Finance - Product Vision

## What we are building

Helm Finance is not a bookkeeping app.

Helm Finance is an AI CFO.

The product helps founders and small business owners understand:

- how much cash they have
- how long runway remains
- what payments are due
- what invoices are expected
- what salaries must be paid
- what financial risks exist
- what actions should be taken today

The user should never feel like they are using accounting software.

The user should feel like they have a personal CFO.

---

## Design Philosophy

We are building:

- Linear-level quality
- Stripe-level simplicity
- Apple-level polish
- Ramp-level finance UX

Never build enterprise-looking dashboards.

Avoid:

- dense tables
- spreadsheet UI
- accounting software appearance

Prefer:

- cards
- summaries
- insights
- actions

---

## Core Screens

### Pulse

Mission control.

Shows:

- total cash
- runway
- receivables
- payables
- salary obligations
- AI insights

---

### Radar

Cash forecast.

Shows:

- 30 day forecast
- best case
- expected case
- worst case

User should understand future cash position in 5 seconds.

---

### Accounts

Bank accounts.

Shows:

- balances
- account health
- account ownership

Eventually connected to banks.

---

### Team

Employees.

Shows:

- employee directory
- monthly payroll
- next payroll obligations

Every employee has:

- name
- position
- salary
- payment date
- status

---

### Invoices

Incoming and outgoing invoices.

Tracks:

- due dates
- payment status
- receivables
- payables

---

## Data Input

Primary input source:

Telegram Bot.

Users send:

- invoices
- receipts
- contracts
- salary information
- notes

AI extracts structured financial data automatically.
